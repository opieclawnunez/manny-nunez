About

I am a board-certified family medicine physician currently serving as a military officer, with active duty service spanning over 20 years. My career has been built on delivering comprehensive primary care in high-tempo, resource-conscious environments — from deployed settings to military treatment facilities across the US.

Upon retirement from military service in August 2026, I am transitioning to outpatient family medicine practice in the greater Phoenix - West Valley area. I am particularly drawn to longitudinal patient relationships, preventive care, and the clinical autonomy that community-based practice offers.

My goal is to join a practice where I can bring the discipline, adaptability, and patient-first mindset developed through military medicine to a civilian outpatient setting — and to build something lasting for the community I call home.
